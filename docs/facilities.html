Gather, Work, Play

Each room is a mood—curated playlists, local art, and lighting that shifts with the day. Explore what makes Vida Hostel Lisbon feel like your collective living room.

Calma Lounge

Sunlit sofa lounge with curated vinyl playlists, bottomless Portuguese coffee, and library selects from local authors.

Winter Garden Atrium

Glass-framed stairwell rich with greenery—ideal for casual meetups, photo ops, and golden-hour sketches.

Private Nook Rooms

Quiet corners for private stays featuring layered textiles, ambient lighting, and aromatherapy turndown.

Brickwork Kitchen & Bar

Communal kitchen doubling as an evening bar with natural wines, tapas nights, and barista-led cold brew in the mornings.

Sunlit Commons

Cowork-ready lounge with fast Wi-Fi, charging rails, whiteboard niches, and a rotating neighborhood zine library.

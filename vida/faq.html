Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions travelers ask most before booking Vida Hostel Lisbon.

Home / FAQ

Stay Experience

Is breakfast included?

Weekend continental breakfast is available as an add-on for €12 per guest. Expect fresh pastries, seasonal fruit, and specialty coffee.

Do you host events?

Yes—weekly rooftop sessions on Fridays, plus art talks and community dinners. Check the News page for schedules.

Services

Is there laundry?

Self-service washers and dryers are located on Level 1 (€5 per load, detergent included).

Can I store luggage?

Complimentary same-day storage is available pre check-in and post check-out. Extended storage is €5 per day, subject to availability.

Policies

What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours prior to arrival. Within 48 hours, 50% of the stay will be charged.

Do you accept group bookings?

Absolutely. We can host groups up to 16 guests with advance notice. Email reservations to customise room blocks and add-ons.

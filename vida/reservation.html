How Booking Works

Our direct booking path keeps things efficient. When you’re ready to confirm, you’ll be redirected to our partner engine in a new tab.

Step 1: Choose your travel dates and occupancy using the calendar selector.
Step 2: Pick your preferred room category and add optional experiences like breakfast or rooftop sessions.
Step 3: Confirm guest details and continue to the external booking channel (opens in a blank tab).
Start Booking

Need tailored help? Email reservations@vidahostellisbon.com with your date range and room type.

Stay Policies

Check-in / Check-out: Check-in from 15:00; check-out by 11:00. Early arrivals may store luggage from 08:00.
Cancellation Window: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival; 50% of stay charged within 48 hours.
Security Deposit: €50 pre-authorization on arrival, released within 24 hours of departure.
Payment Methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay.

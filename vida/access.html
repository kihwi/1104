Access & Arrival

Nestled between Chiado and Bairro Alto, Vida Hostel Lisbon is your base for tram rides, gallery crawls, and sunset miradouros.

Home / Access

Where to Find Us

Rua Nova da Vida 27, 1200-215 Lisboa, Portugal.

Metro & Tram

From Lisbon Airport (LIS): take the Red Line to Alameda, transfer to the Green Line towards Cais do Sodré, and exit at Baixa-Chiado. Walk 8 minutes uphill towards Bairro Alto.

Tram 28 stop “Chiado” is a 2-minute walk; purchase 24h passes at any metro kiosk.

Rideshare & Car

Off-peak rideshare takes 20 minutes from the airport. For drivers, we recommend the underground garage at Largo Camões (400m) with 24-hour security.

Neighborhood Highlights

Steps from independent galleries, Bairro Alto studios, and the São Pedro de Alcântara viewpoint for sweeping river views.
